Our Vision

We aspire to enlighten individuals about the causes and effects of substance abuse. As a team, we visualize a world where people are aware of the ill effects of substances and what it does to their body. We aim to reach out to the masses so that people who are not able to help themselves to withdraw from using various intoxicants can get adequate assistance and support. Our project objective is to understand the risk factors that predispose children and adolescents to substance use disorders and the protective factors that lead to resilience and limit substance abuse and addiction.

What We Do

We ensure healthy lives after analysing what harmful substances do to our body and how we can withdraw from these wrong habits.
